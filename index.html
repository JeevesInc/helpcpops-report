Section 5 · Glossary
Key terms explained
/helpcp
Slack slash command that opens the request form. Each submission becomes a Zendesk ticket auto-routed to the right owner.
Deposit not reflected
Funds sent by the client haven't yet appeared in the Jeeves wallet — usually settlement/on-chain latency rather than a lost transaction.
Payout
Money leaving the client's wallet to a supplier or beneficiary (wire/SWIFT, fiat or stablecoin conversion).
Bridge
Stablecoin payments partner. "Offboarded by Bridge" means the partner deactivated the client and Jeeves must request reactivation.
RF
Payments rail/processor used for certain payouts. An "internal RF error" is a fault on the partner's side, not Jeeves's.
MT103
The SWIFT message for a cross-border wire. A typo in the beneficiary name can block crediting and require a formal amendment.
Allowlist
Marking a client/merchant as trusted so legitimate, recurring transactions aren't blocked by fraud or "unusual activity" rules.
DELTA
Acceptable price variance on a stablecoin deposit. When the client accepts DELTA, deposits can be auto-credited without holding for price checks.
Logistics (cards)
Physical card production and delivery — printing, shipping (e.g. Domesa), address corrections and "pending" card requests.
Off-flow request
A request typed as a normal Slack message instead of through /helpcp, so no ticket is auto-created and there's no audit trail.
